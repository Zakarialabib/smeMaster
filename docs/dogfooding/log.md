# SMEMaster Dogfooding Log

Welcome to the SMEMaster internal dogfooding program. The goal of this exercise is to
use SMEMaster as your primary email, contacts, tasks, and calendar tool for **7
consecutive days** and capture honest, unfiltered feedback.

The team that builds it should be the first to use it. Your daily notes here directly
shape the v1.0.0 release.

## How to use this log

1. Fill in the **Day N** block every day before you stop working.
2. Be specific — "compose was slow" is less useful than "compose took ~6 s to open
   when 12 drafts were open in the background".
3. Bugs go in the **What broke** section. Even small ones. Even "the toast animation
   stuttered once".
4. The **"Would I switch back?"** rating is the most important number we collect. If
   the answer drops below 4 for two days in a row, escalate to the gate owner.
