# User Guide — Company & ERP

SMEMaster organises your business data per **company**. The active company is
shown in the top bar; switch it from the **company switcher** dropdown.

## What a company is

A company is the tenant that owns your contacts, clients, invoices, workflow
rules, and ERP books (ledger, journal, stock, wallet, roles). Its Moroccan
**ICE** number is shown next to the name.

## Switch companies

1. Click the **company chip** in the top bar (shows initials + name + ICE).
2. Pick another company from the list and click **Switch**.

> **Demo note:** the shipped build includes mock companies to illustrate
> switching. Real multi-company accounts (separate data, separate sign-in) are a
> Platform-tier feature.

## ERP

Open **ERP** to see the financial side of the active company: financial reports,
the journal, stock/inventory, the wallet, and role-based access (RBAC) roles.
Everything you see there belongs to the currently active company.
