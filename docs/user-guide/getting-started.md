# SMEMaster — Getting Started Guide

Welcome to SMEMaster, the all-in-one business management desktop app for small and medium enterprises. This guide will get you from installation to sending your first email in under 5 minutes.

## Installation

### Windows
1. Download the latest `.msi` or `.exe` installer from the [releases page](https://github.com/smemaster/smemaster/releases)
2. Run the installer and follow the prompts
3. Launch SMEMaster from the Start Menu or desktop shortcut

### macOS
1. Download the latest `.dmg` file from the [releases page](https://github.com/smemaster/smemaster/releases)
2. Open the DMG and drag SMEMaster to your Applications folder
3. Launch SMEMaster from Applications

### Linux
1. Download the `.AppImage` or `.deb` file from the [releases page](https://github.com/smemaster/smemaster/releases)
2. For AppImage: `chmod +x SMEMaster-*.AppImage && ./SMEMaster-*.AppImage`
3. For Debian/Ubuntu: `sudo dpkg -i smemaster_*.deb`

## First Launch

1. On first launch, you'll see the onboarding wizard
2. Follow the 4 steps: AI features, Email sync, Contacts, and Notifications
3. After onboarding, you'll be prompted to add your first email account

## Adding Your Email Account

1. Click "Add Account" in the sidebar
2. Choose your email provider (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, or Other)
3. For Gmail/Outlook: Click "Sign in with Google/Microsoft" and authorize
4. For other providers: Enter your IMAP/SMTP settings manually
5. Your emails will start syncing automatically

## Sending Your First Email

1. Click the "Compose" button (pencil icon) in the top-left
2. Enter the recipient's email address
3. Write a subject and message
4. Click "Send" — your email will be sent immediately
